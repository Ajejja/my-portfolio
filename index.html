Information About me

Hello! My name is Mohamed, but all my Japanese friends call me Moha. I’m a 23-year-old student from Tunisia, currently pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering at Ishikawa College of Technology. Starting next April, I’ll be embarking on a new journey at Kanazawa University to study Information Technology.

I have a diverse range of hobbies that include free diving, cooking, listening to music, fishing, and traveling. My love for making friends has made me a very sociable person, always eager to connect with new people.

My first internship at jig.jp, I developed some Android applications using Kotlin. During my internship at jig.jp, I worked on a fascinating project themed "SLEEP," where I created a web application for sharing dreams. This application featured tagging, commenting, and a homepage showcasing all shared dreams. To ensure secure authentication, I implemented a DID login technique based on blockchain technology.

Following this, I completed an internship in the UK, where I had the opportunity to interact with IT engineers who encouraged me to learn Flask and Python. This inspiration led me to develop a school project website that enables students to share news and internship information, boasting features similar to the dream-sharing app.

Currently, I am a long time intern at Sigmai, where I am involved in designing and developing the user interface for a web application tailored for a home nursing company.
